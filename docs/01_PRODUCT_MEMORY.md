# 01 — PRODUCT MEMORY

## What was already established
The central idea is not merely to list organisations.
The central idea is to solve the fragmented transition after school.

## The emotional-operational nature of the problem
This is not a purely informational search.
Families are making consequential decisions under uncertainty.
The product must therefore combine:
- clarity
- practical filtering
- trust
- orientation
- next-step confidence

## Why the first release should be a website
A web-first product is the right first step because it is:
- easier to validate
- easier to share
- easier to refine
- better for early discovery
- appropriate before a heavier operational layer exists

## Product direction agreed in the work so far
The product should begin with:
- search
- matching
- comparison logic
- provider profiles
- clear categories
- a calm decision experience

Later it may include:
- verified provider profiles
- availability or waitlist status
- request flows
- saved shortlist
- status tracking
- document / checklist utilities

## The core promise
One structured path for the transition after school.

## The strongest strategic framing
Do not frame it as “a platform for disability providers”.
Do frame it as a navigation and matching platform for families after school.

## The first release should feel like
- a real product, not a concept deck
- premium and restrained
- launchable with placeholder content
- prepared for real data later
