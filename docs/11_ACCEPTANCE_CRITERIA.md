# 11 — ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

Codex is done when all of the following are true.

## Product clarity
- The site clearly positions Weiterpfad as a transition platform after school.
- The site does not read as a generic directory.
- The matching logic is visible and understandable.
- The provider strategy is credible.

## Design quality
- The site looks premium and calm.
- The site is not overloaded.
- The site does not look AI-generic.
- The site preserves the strongest visual qualities of the current homepage.

## Structure
- All required routes exist.
- Navigation is coherent.
- Components are reusable and clean.
- Mock data is separated from pages.

## Code quality
- Imports are valid.
- TypeScript is clean.
- No broken pages.
- No unnecessary libraries.
- The codebase is easy to continue from.

## UX
- Filters or controls are keyboard-usable.
- Contrast is good.
- Mobile is solid.
- Contact flow looks intentional even without backend logic.

## Business credibility
- Families can understand the product.
- Providers can understand why they would participate later.
- The site feels suitable for early partners or funders to review.
