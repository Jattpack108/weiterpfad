# 00 — SINGLE SOURCE OF TRUTH

## Product name
Weiterpfad

## One-sentence definition
Weiterpfad is a transition platform for families of young people with disabilities who need a clearer path to work, living, day programs and counselling after school.

## Core strategic insight
The landscape is not empty.
The landscape is fragmented.
The gap is not the absence of information.
The gap is the absence of a calm, usable, family-facing decision product.

## The real problem
Families often face a scattered search process at exactly the moment when they need structure.
The search is spread across portals, provider sites, advisory pages, phone calls, lists and word of mouth.
Information is hard to compare.
Terminology is confusing.
The next step is often unclear.
The result is uncertainty, delay and overload.

## The right product response
Weiterpfad should not try to win by being the biggest directory.
Weiterpfad should win by being the clearest transition layer after school.

That means:
- unify relevant option types in one experience
- reduce cognitive overload
- show what is relevant now
- make matching tangible early
- allow comparison and shortlisting later
- grow into provider verification and availability updates over time

## Primary audience
Families of young people with disabilities in the transition phase after school.

## Secondary audiences
- providers and organisations
- early partners
- possible funders
- future regional ecosystem actors

## What the first release is
The first release is:
- a premium web-first website
- a serious front door for trust, validation and early conversations
- partly narrative, partly product demonstration
- visually launch-ready
- strategically clear
- technically lightweight enough to evolve fast

## What the first release is not
The first release is not:
- a full transactional marketplace
- a giant live database product
- a backend-heavy operations platform
- a native mobile app
- a generic government-information clone
- a flashy AI startup landing page

## Product categories that must stay visible
- Work
- Living
- Day program / day structure
- Counselling

## Positioning statement
Weiterpfad is a structured transition platform after school for families who need clarity across work, living, day structure and counselling — without getting lost in fragmented searches, provider jargon and disconnected next steps.

## Simple thesis for the site
A better next step becomes possible when the search feels calm, structured and comparable.

## Brand character
- calm
- serious
- premium
- warm without sentimentality
- modern but not trendy
- highly legible
- trustworthy
- low-noise

## Design truth
The site must not look AI-generated, generic, overbuilt or visually loud.
It should feel like a premium, humane digital product built for a sensitive life decision.

## Homepage role
The homepage must do four things fast:
1. explain what Weiterpfad is
2. show why it matters
3. make matching visible
4. prove taste and credibility

## Structural truth about the market
Existing offerings already cover fragments of the problem:
- information portals
- advisory search
- provider websites
- free-place / capacity signals in isolated contexts
- partial maps and lists

This means Weiterpfad should not claim category creation.
It should claim category integration and decision quality.

## Early product logic
Phase 1 does not need live integrations.
Phase 1 needs:
- structured discovery
- coherent categories
- visible filters
- plausible ranking logic
- provider-ready data structure

## Long-term product logic
The long-term win is not just search.
The long-term win is a trusted transition workflow:
- matching
- comparing
- saving options
- provider verification
- updates and availability signals
- operational usefulness without bureaucratic overload

## Non-negotiables for Codex
- preserve the strengths of the current homepage
- refactor it into reusable components
- create all required routes
- keep the visual language calm and expensive
- write credible placeholder content
- avoid filler and fake product complexity
- leave a clean architecture that can grow

## Final quality bar
This should look like a site that could be shown tomorrow to:
- parents
- providers
- advisory partners
- potential backers

and still feel intentional, serious and ready.
